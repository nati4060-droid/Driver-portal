The Problem

Four channels. Zero source of truth.

In a national-scale rail operation running hundreds of drivers, the daily work schedule is distributed in parallel across multiple communication channels. Real-time updates are sometimes relayed verbally, with no unified acknowledgment mechanism. A clear opportunity for operational improvement.
